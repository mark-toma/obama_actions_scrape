  Memorandum for the Heads of Executive Departments and Agencies - Subject: Government Contracting

  
  

  
      
  
  
  
        
                THE WHITE HOUSE  Office of the Press Secretary  ________________________________________    For Immediate Release                                 March 4, 2009  Memorandum for the Heads of Executive Departments and Agencies  Subject: Government Contracting  The Federal Government has an overriding obligation to American taxpayers. It should perform its functions efficiently and effectively while ensuring that its actions result in the best value for the taxpayers.
